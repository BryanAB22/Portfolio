Biography

As a first-generation Hispanic student, I am currently pursuing a major in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics at Tyler Junior College (TJC). This summer, I will be joining the University of Texas at Tyler (UT Tyler) as a full-time student, with plans to graduate in December 2024 or, at the latest, May 2025. My ambitious timeline aims to complete my degree a year earlier than initially anticipated. Following my bachelor's degree, I intend to further my education by obtaining a master's degree in Computer Science. Though I do not have any direct experience in the field, I have gained invaluable skills through my work in the food industry since the age of 14. Through various leadership roles, I have honed my abilities in teamwork, communication, stress management, and handling high-pressure situations. My experience managing teams of 10 to 15 people demonstrates my aptitude for leadership and adaptability. I am confident that these skills, combined with my passion for computer science, will make me a valuable addition to any team. Eager to gain real-world experience that cannot be found in a classroom setting, I am excited about the opportunity to participate in an internship and further develop my expertise in the field.
